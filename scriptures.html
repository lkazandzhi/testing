Make your scripture study a ROAD to revelation!

This verse-by-verse guide has over 5000 scripture study questions that are:

R

Related to the Gospel

The goal of every question is to increase faith, encourage repentance, lead to greater covenant-keeping, invite the Holy Ghost, and help you endure to the end.

O

Open-ended

Open-ended questions encourage reflection and discussion. They are far better than closed-ended questions that require a simple answer and little thought.

A

Application-focused

When questions are applicable to our lives, they help us find personal meaning in the gospel and be "doers of the word, and not hearers only" (James 1:22).

D

Deep

Deep questions lead to a deeper understanding of the gospel and a more sure testimony. Responses to these questions go far beyond the "Sunday school answers"!

The Isaiah Chapters
(Click to flip card)

2 Nephi 15:5
 To destroy the vineyard, the Lord only needed to withhold His protective power from it. How does sinning "turn off" the power of God in our lives? How does repentance turn it back on?

Abinadi's Message
(Click to flip card)

Mosiah 12:31-32
 How would you respond to this similar question: "Doth salvation come by reading scriptures, saying prayers, and going to church?" Why or why not?

Jacob's Allegory of the Olive Trees
(Click to flip card)

Jacob 5:20
 The Lord spent considerable time nourishing the various branches of Israel, and they brought forth considerable fruit. Up to this point in your life, how has the Lord nourished you, both physically and spiritually? What fruits have you brought forth to Him in return?

The Story of the Anti-Nephi-Lehies
(Click to flip card)

Alma 24:8
 The conversion of the Anti-Nephi-Lehies allowed them to repair and strengthen their relationships with the Nephites. How can the gospel of Jesus Christ heal relationships, especially in marriages and families? How has the gospel blessed the relationships in your life?

Which gospel topics interest you?

God's Love
(Click to flip card)

1 Nephi 11:22-23
 When was the last time you felt this kind of love from your Heavenly Father? What stops us (and you personally) from feeling it? What helps us (and you personally) to feel it?

Covenants
(Click to flip card)

Enos 1:17
 What covenants have you made with God, and what blessings do they entitle you to? How does your faith in those promises bring rest to your soul?

Trials
(Click to flip card)

Alma 2:27
 Sometimes our trials can seem "as numerous as the sands of the sea." What trials are you facing in your life now? What do you stand in need of, spiritually and temporally, that you could ask your Heavenly Father for? How much faith do you have that He can help you with these things?

Parenting
(Click to flip card)

Mosiah 12:28-29
 The priests claimed to teach the law of Moses but they themselves didn't keep it. If you are a parent, is there anything that you are trying to teach your children but that you yourself aren't a good example of? If so, how can you improve? In what ways are you setting a good example of what you teach?
